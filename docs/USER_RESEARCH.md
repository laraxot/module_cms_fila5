# Cms Module - User Research

**Module:** Cms  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Product Team

---

## Research Goals

1. Understand content creator workflows
2. Identify pain points with current process
3. Validate CMS UX
4. Determine training needs

---

## Research Questions

| ID | Question | Priority |
|----|----------|----------|
| RQ1 | What content do teams create? | P0 |
| RQ2 | What are current pain points? | P0 |
| RQ3 | What features are essential? | P1 |
| RQ4 | What training is needed? | P1 |

---

## Methodology

- **Interviews:** 10 content creators
- **Workflow Analysis:** Current process mapping
- **Usability Testing:** CMS prototype testing
- **Surveys:** 50+ potential users

---

## Participant Profiles

### Marketing Manager
- **Needs:** Campaign pages, landing pages
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Technical Level:** Low

### Content Writer
- **Needs:** Article creation, editing
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Technical Level:** Medium

### Support Lead
- **Needs:** Help documentation, FAQs
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Technical Level:** Low

---

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: Speed Important
Users want to publish content quickly.

### Finding 2: Preview Critical
Users need to see content before publishing.

### Finding 3: Collaboration Needed
Multiple users often work on same content.

### Finding 4: Training Essential
Non-technical users need onboarding.

---

## Recommendations

### Immediate
- Create intuitive page builder
- Add real-time preview
- Implement basic workflows

### Long-Term
- Build collaboration features
- Create video training library
- Add content analytics
